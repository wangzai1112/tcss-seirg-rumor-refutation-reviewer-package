tables, and figure source tables produced by this project may be used for
scholarly verification and reuse with attribution to the archived repository,
the manuscript version, and the SHA256 manifest.

If redistribution permission is unclear, the public archive should retain only
aggregate non-identifying outputs, source snapshots where permitted, access
logs, checksums where allowed, download instructions, and processing scripts.

personally identifiable or source-restricted records should not be released.

## Manuscript Boundary

External data are used as proxies, timing anchors, calibration aids, or
external plausibility checks. They should not be described as full external
validation of the SEIRG model or as causal evidence that real-world policy
action reduced rumor propagation.
